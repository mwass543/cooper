Your Coastal Escape in Kilifi

Rooted in authentic coastal tradition, thoughtfully designed for today’s traveler. Makuti Villas is more than just a place to stay — it’s your space to unwind, breathe deeply, and reconnect. Each room blends natural textures, open airy layouts, and calming interiors to create an atmosphere that feels grounded yet refined. From lazy mornings with coffee on your veranda to golden-hour evenings under the stars, every detail encourages rest and those quiet moments of joy. Whether you’re coming for a weekend retreat, a family getaway, or an extended stay in Kilifi, Makuti Villas offers a warm, welcoming environment where comfort, privacy, and tranquility come together seamlessly..

At Makuti Villas, every stay is designed to offer comfort, flexibility, and a touch of coastal charm. Guests can select from our Single and Double Standard Rooms, stylish Deluxe Rooms, or spacious 2-Bedroom and 3-Bedroom Self-Catering Villas perfect for longer stays or group travel. Our accommodation rates include bed and breakfast options, with additional arrangements available for extra guests and convenient day room use. Families are warmly welcomed with special considerations for young children and those between six and twelve years. Explore our rack rates below and discover the relaxing Makuti experience waiting for you.
