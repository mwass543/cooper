Our Amenities

Experience the best of Makuti Villas with a blend of relaxation, luxury, and adventure. Lounge by our sparkling swimming pool, enjoy serene walks in our lush tropical gardens, and indulge in flavorful dishes and refreshing drinks at our bar & restaurant. Whether seeking quiet moments, family fun, or a space to connect with friends, our amenities offer something for every guest to make your stay unforgettable.

Refreshing Pool

Take a dip or lounge in style by our pristine pool. Perfect for cooling off on sunny Kilifi days or enjoying a relaxed evening swim.

Lush Garden

Wander through vibrant gardens filled with tropical flora. Ideal for morning strolls, photoshoots, or hosting intimate outdoor gatherings.

Kids Playground

A safe and fun outdoor playground designed especially for our younger guests. Perfect for play, laughter, and adventure while parents relax nearby.

Bar & Restaurant

Savor fresh, locally-inspired dishes and refreshing drinks in our cozy bar & restaurant. Perfect for brunches, dinners, or evening cocktails.
